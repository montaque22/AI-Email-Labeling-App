# Emailable

Emailable is an email organization and automation application that uses artificial intelligence to classify messages, apply labels or move messages into folders, draft replies, and create reusable rules from uncertain decisions.

It provides one place to connect multiple email accounts, define a consistent set of categories, review how the AI handled messages, and expose the same workflows to external automation systems through APIs, webhooks, MCP, and Home Assistant.

## Who Emailable is for

Emailable is useful for people and teams who:

- Manage email across multiple accounts or providers.
- Want AI-assisted organization without giving up review and control.
- Need consistent labels or folders across connected accounts.
- Build automations in n8n, Home Assistant, or another API-capable system.
- Want reusable prompts and confidence thresholds instead of duplicating AI configuration in every workflow.

## Why use Emailable

Email automation often becomes fragmented. Labels live in one provider, rules live in another, prompts are copied between automation tools, and uncertain AI decisions are difficult to review.

Emailable acts as the source of truth for those workflows. It can:

- Synchronize categories across connected email accounts.
- Apply one best-fit label or folder to an email.
- Create pending rules when a classification needs human review.
- Poll connected accounts or accept email data through APIs and MCP.
- Draft and send replies using the user's connected accounts.
- Keep AI prompts, confidence settings, metrics, and logs together.

The result is a workflow that can be automated while remaining visible, editable, and accountable.
